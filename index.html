The SOC CodeOut 2021 is a CTF'd style competition open to all current students in SP SOC. The competition is designed to encourage a problem-solving mindset in students through the use of computational thinking questions.
The use of programming to solve the questions is encouraged. No prior knowledge of any domains is expected in order to solve the questions.

Rules:

  1. DO NOT attack or probe the Scoring System and/or our Networks
  2. DO NOT attack any challenges (Including the Challenge Server)
  3. Respect your fellow competitors, do not attack, insult or otherwise negatively impact other participants or teams
  4. DO NOT delete or modify flags
  5. Sharing flags, solutions or hints is prohibited. Cheating is no fun!
  6. Brute-forcing of flags will not be tolerated
  7. DO report any bugs or gitches found
  8. The Organisers' decisions are final
  9. Most importantly, have fun, explore and learn!
